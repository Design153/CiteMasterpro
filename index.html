What is Referencing?

Referencing is the practice of giving credit to the original authors of the sources you use in your work. It allows readers to trace the origins of your information and supports the validity of your arguments. Proper referencing is required in academic essays, research papers, reports, and even professional documents.

Why Citation Matters

Avoid Plagiarism

Giving proper credit to the original authors prevents accidental plagiarism, ensuring ethical writing.

Enhance Credibility

Well-referenced work is more reliable and authoritative, building trust with your audience.

Provide Evidence

Citing sources supports your ideas with valid research, strengthening your arguments.

Locate Sources Easily

Proper citations allow readers to find and verify the sources you used, enhancing transparency.
